Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from prospective clients.

How quickly can you get started on my project?
Most projects begin within 3–5 business days of signing. Simple services like SEO and marketing can launch the same week. Larger implementations such as custom web development or EHR integrations typically take 2–3 weeks for onboarding and planning.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Yes — we serve clients globally. Our team operates across multiple time zones, and our 24/7 support model ensures continuous coverage regardless of where your business is located. We currently serve clients in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and the Middle East.
What makes Skyworld revenue different from other IT agencies?
Unlike generalist agencies, we specialize deeply in specific verticals — particularly healthcare IT and e-commerce. Our integrated service model means every service communicates with the others: your SEO data informs your paid ads, your CRM data improves your support — all under one strategic roof with shared reporting.
How do you measure and report results?
Every engagement includes a dedicated dashboard with KPIs agreed upfront. We provide monthly performance reports, quarterly business reviews, and real-time access to key metrics. You'll always know exactly what we're doing and what it's delivering.
What are your contract terms?
We offer flexible engagement models — month-to-month retainers, quarterly agreements, and annual contracts (with discounts). Project-based work is quoted per scope. We believe in earning your business each month through results, not locking you in with punitive contracts.
Can I mix and match services?
Absolutely. Our solution packages are a starting point — you can combine any of our 12 core services in any configuration that suits your business. Many clients start with one service and expand over time as trust and results build.
